Year 8 Religious Education

In Year 8 Religious Education, students explore foundational concepts of various world religions, including Christianity, Islam, Buddhism, and Hinduism. They learn about key beliefs, practices, and historical figures in these religions, fostering an understanding of diverse cultural and spiritual perspectives.

NCEA Level 1 Religious Education

NCEA Level 1 Religious Studies introduces students to the academic study of religion. They explore the origins and significance of different religious beliefs and practices, as well as their impact on individuals and communities. Students may also analyze religious texts, rituals, and ethical dilemmas to develop critical thinking skills.

Last updated 42 mins ago
NCEA Level 1
NCEA Level 2 Religious Education

At NCEA Level 2, students delve further into the study of religion as a cultural and social phenomenon. They examine the role of religion in shaping historical events and societal values. The course may involve analyzing religious art, literature, and philosophical writings to gain a deeper insight into the complexities of faith and spirituality.

Last updated 37 mins ago

NCEA Level 3 Religious Education

NCEA Level 3 Religious Studies offers an advanced exploration of religious themes and issues. Students critically analyze religious beliefs and practices, engaging with philosophical debates and ethical challenges. They might study religious responses to contemporary global issues, religious diversity, and the intersections of faith and identity.
